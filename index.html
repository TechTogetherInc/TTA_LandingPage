About TechTogether

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized individuals in interdisciplinary fields of technology. We do this by hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing professional development resources.

TechTogether was founded in 2017 as SheHacks by 13 college students across Boston. What was once a one-time event is now a national organization that serves individuals around the world.

About TechTogether Atlanta

TechTogether Atlanta is Atlanta‚Äôs collegiate and high school hackathon for gender-marginalized individuals. With a team from all over the world, Tech Together Atlanta is striving to provide support and opportunities virtually to gender-marginalized hackers.
